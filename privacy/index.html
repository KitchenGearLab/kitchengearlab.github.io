Privacy policy

How KitchenGearLab handles data, cookies, and analytics.

Summary: We collect basic analytics to understand what content helps readers. We do not sell personal information.

What we collect

  • Analytics data: approximate location (city/region), device/browser type, pages viewed, and interactions.
  • Cookies: may be used by analytics and affiliate partners to measure referrals.
  • Voluntary info: if you contact us, you share what you type (like your email and message).

How we use data

  • Improve content clarity and usefulness.
  • Understand which guides people actually read and where they get stuck.
  • Maintain site security and performance.

Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to measure traffic and content performance. We enable IP anonymization where supported. Analytics data helps us improve pages and keep information updated.

Affiliate links

Some links may be affiliate links. If you click and purchase, we may earn a commission. This does not increase your price. See Disclosure.

Third parties

Third-party services (analytics, affiliate networks, or embedded content) may set cookies or collect usage data under their own policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy documentation if you want details.

Your choices

  • You can limit cookies in your browser settings (note: some site features may be affected).
  • You can use private browsing modes to reduce stored cookies.

Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us via the details on the About page.
