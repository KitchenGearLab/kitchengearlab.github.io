Privacy policy

What we measure, what we don’t, and your choices.

Summary: We use basic analytics to improve content. We do not sell personal information.

Scope

KitchenGearLab is a content website. We do not offer user accounts and we do not provide a public contact channel. This policy explains what information may be processed when you browse the site.

What we collect

  • Analytics data: page views, approximate location (city/region), device/browser type, and interactions (e.g., clicks).
  • Cookies: may be used by analytics and affiliate partners to measure traffic and referrals.

What we do not collect

  • No user accounts or profiles.
  • No direct submission of personal information through forms on this site.
  • No sale of personal information.

How we use data

  • Improve content clarity and usefulness.
  • Understand what readers engage with so we can update guides and comparisons.
  • Maintain site security, reliability, and performance.

Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to measure site usage and content performance. This helps us understand which pages are useful and where to improve. We configure analytics in a privacy-minded way (for example, IP anonymization where supported).

Affiliate links

Some links may be affiliate links. If you click and purchase, we may earn a commission. This does not increase your price. See Disclosure.

Third parties

Third-party services (analytics providers, affiliate networks, or embedded content) may set cookies or process usage data under their own policies.

Your choices

  • You can limit cookies in your browser settings (note: some site features may be affected).
  • You can use private browsing modes to reduce stored cookies.
  • You can block analytics scripts using browser settings or extensions.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. When we do, we’ll revise the “Last updated” date below.

Informational only. This page is a general summary and not legal advice.
