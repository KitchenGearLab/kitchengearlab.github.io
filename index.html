Trust & methodology

How we evaluate gear (and how to use our work)

KitchenGearLab is built around clear criteria and repeatable checks. We describe what we did and what we observed, so you can decide whether a recommendation fits your cooking style, budget, and space.

What we look for

  • Usability: controls, ergonomics, readability, and day-to-day friction.
  • Performance: consistent results under typical home conditions (varies by model).
  • Cleanup: residue traps, dishwasher practicality, surfaces that clean easily.
  • Durability cues: build quality signals and common failure points.
  • Transparency: we avoid repeating claims we can’t verify.

We keep disclosures clear and separate. See Disclosure.
