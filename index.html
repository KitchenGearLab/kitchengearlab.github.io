Trust & methodology

How to read our guides and use them well.

We evaluate kitchen gear using repeatable checks and clear criteria. Our goal is not to crown a universal “best”, but to explain trade-offs so you can decide what fits your kitchen.

  • Usability: controls, ergonomics, day-to-day friction.
  • Performance: consistency under normal home use.
  • Cleanup: residue traps, dishwasher practicality.
  • Durability cues: realistic longevity signals.
  • Transparency: no unverified claims.
