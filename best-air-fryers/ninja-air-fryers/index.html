How we evaluate

Repeatable checks. Clear criteria. Trade-offs explained.

We don’t crown a universal “best”. We explain what changes the day-to-day experience so you can choose what fits your kitchen.

  • Usability: controls, ergonomics, day-to-day friction.
  • Performance: consistency under normal home use.
  • Cleanup: residue traps, dishwasher practicality.
  • Durability cues: realistic longevity signals (fit/finish, hinges, coatings).
  • Transparency: no unverified claims.
